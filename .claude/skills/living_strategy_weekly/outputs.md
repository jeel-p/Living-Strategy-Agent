# Outputs — Living Strategy weekly run (intelligence + calendars)

Default path for **`/living-strategy-plugin:weekly-brief`**. Keep an **executive skim** under ~3 minutes: tight bullets in **Executive snapshot** and **What's moving now**; details can be scannable tables.

Use this order. Do **not** skip sections; if data is missing, say **Insufficient data** and continue.

---

## 1. Title line

`# [Week headline — single most important insight for agility this week]`

---

## 2. Executive snapshot (skim block)

- **Domain / offer:** [one line]
- **Markets:** [line]
- **Top 3 moves externally** (timing): [bullets]
- **Top implication for our audience:** [one line]

---

## 3. What's moving now (timing — recency first)

`## What's moving now`

Bullets: **only** signals with **recent** evidence (last ~7–14 days preferred; say **older** when tools show only that). Tag source type: `[News]` `[Reddit]` `[Trends]` `[SOT]` `[Social]`.

---

## 4. Second-order implications

`## Second-order implications`

For **2–4** items from §3: **If [event/signal] → then [likely change in buyer behavior / risk / opportunity]** for our **target audience**. This is not channel tactics yet—it's **cause → consumer effect**.

---

## 5. Signal map (where we looked)

`## Signal map`

Short subsections (each with bullets + **links** when available):

- **Reddit & Quora** (voice-of-customer; use **`reddit_quora_market_intelligence`** patterns)
- **Search / Google Trends-style** (directional; use **`google_trends_report`** on URL/keywords when applicable)
- **Live news & events** (search + fetch)
- **Primary SOT / regulators** (MoF, FTA, central bank, free zone—**user `custom_sources`**)
- **Social / other** (if used)

If a lane was skipped (no tools / no time), state **Not run this cycle**.

---

## 6. Strategic read

`## Strategic read`

**2–3 sentences:** synthesis for **this brand** now—not a list of inputs.

---

## 7. Trend signals (ranked by urgency)

`## Trend signals (ranked by urgency)`

Per signal: what it is; why it matters **here**; time window; **HIGH | MEDIUM | WATCH**; links.

## 10. Audience value summary

`## Audience value summary`

**1 short paragraph:** what **useful takeaway** this week's materials deliver for the target audience (clarity, compliance path, risk reduction, timing).

---

## 11. Priority actions

`## Priority actions`

- `### Do immediately (next 48 hours)` — **One** action (channel + asset + why now).
- `### Do this week` — 2–3 actions.
- `### Watch and hold` — 1–2 triggers: **If [X] by [date], then [Y].**

---

## 12. Budget and channel guidance

`## Budget and channel guidance`

Directional **LinkedIn vs blog vs paid vs email** (or relative priority if no budget numbers).

---

## 13. What NOT to do this week

`## What NOT to do this week`

1–2 items + why (avoid generic awareness; avoid off-domain noise).

---

## 14. Next week setup

`## Next week setup`

One preparatory action for **faster** content next week (e.g. pillar page, UTM, creative brief).

---

## 15. Sources & research basis

`## Sources & research basis`

Bullet list: **`[label](url)`** — what it supported. Include Reddit/Quora threads, news, SOT pages, trend searches.

---

## Quality bar

- **Recency** explicit in §3 and §5.
- **Second-order** in §4 is non-negotiable when §3 has signals.
- **Calendars** must tie to **signals**, not generic topics.
- **Links** for material claims when URLs exist.
